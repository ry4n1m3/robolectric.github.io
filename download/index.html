Downloads

Robolectric releases are available on Maven Central.

If you’re not using Maven or Gradle to build your project, you’ll probably want to grab the latest robolectric-X.X.X-jar-with-dependencies.jar from Maven Central.

Snapshot builds for 2.3 can be obtained from Sonatype

Pre-2.0 versions of Robolectric are available on Maven Central under the com.pivotallabs group ID.
